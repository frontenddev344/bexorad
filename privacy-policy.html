Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to Bexorad.com. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or purchase products from us.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you place an order, create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact our customer support team, we may collect information including:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Billing Address
  • Shipping Address
  • Payment Information (processed securely through third-party payment providers)
  • Order History
  • Account Login Credentials

Automatically Collected Information

When you browse our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Information
  • Operating System
  • Pages Visited
  • Products Viewed
  • Time Spent on Website
  • Referral Source
  • Shopping Cart Activity
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This information helps us improve website functionality, security, and customer experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide a better shopping experience and improve our services. Your information may be used to:

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Deliver purchased products
  • Provide customer support
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Process refunds and returns
  • Send shipping confirmations
  • Improve website performance
  • Personalize product recommendations
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized activity
  • Send promotional emails (only if you have subscribed)
  • Improve customer service
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Bexorad uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your shopping cart
  • Save account preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Display relevant product recommendations
  • Measure marketing campaign performance

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some website features may not function properly.

4. Marketing Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we may send emails regarding:

  • New product launches
  • Exclusive promotions
  • Seasonal collections
  • Special discounts
  • Company updates
  • Sports apparel trends

You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the “Unsubscribe” link included in our emails.

5. Payment Security

We do not store your complete payment card information on our servers. Payments are securely processed through trusted third-party payment providers using encrypted technology that complies with industry security standards.

Accepted payment methods may include:

  • Visa
  • Mastercard
  • American Express
  • Discover
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Other secure payment providers

6. Order Information

To complete your purchase, we collect information necessary for:

  • Order processing
  • Shipping
  • Delivery updates
  • Customer support
  • Warranty requests
  • Returns and refunds

Order information may be retained for accounting, tax, and customer service purposes.

7. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only with trusted service providers necessary to operate our business, including:

  • Shipping carriers
  • Payment processors
  • Website hosting providers
  • Email marketing services
  • Analytics providers
  • Fraud prevention services
  • Customer support platforms

These service providers may only use your information for the services they perform on our behalf.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. These websites operate independently and have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy practices before providing personal information.

Bexorad is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

9. Google Analytics and Advertising

We may use analytics and advertising tools to better understand website performance and customer behavior. These services may collect information regarding:

  • Website visits
  • Device information
  • Browser activity
  • Shopping behavior
  • Advertising interactions

This information helps us improve user experience and optimize our marketing efforts.

10. Data Security

Protecting your information is a priority. We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

These measures include:

  • SSL Encryption
  • Secure Payment Processing
  • Restricted Access Controls
  • Firewall Protection
  • Regular Security Monitoring

While we strive to protect your information, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain privacy rights, including the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Update your account information
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your personal information where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

12. Children's Privacy

Bexorad does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that personal information has been submitted by a child without appropriate parental consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information.

13. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Complete transactions
  • Provide customer support
  • Meet legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements
  • Improve our services

When information is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it where appropriate.

14. International Customers

Bexorad serves customers from multiple countries. If you access our website from outside Canada, your information may be processed or stored in countries where our service providers operate. By using our website, you consent to such transfers where permitted by applicable law.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our business practices, legal requirements, or website functionality. The updated version will always display the latest effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

16. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us.
