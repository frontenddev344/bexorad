Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to Bexorad.com. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or purchase products from us.

Automatically Collected Information

When you browse our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP Address

  • Shopping Cart Activity
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This information helps us improve website functionality, security, and customer experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide a better shopping experience and improve our services. Your information may be used to:

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Deliver purchased products
  • Provide customer support
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Process refunds and returns
  • Send shipping confirmations
  • Improve website performance
  • Personalize product recommendations
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized activity
  • Send promotional emails (only if you have subscribed)
  • Improve customer service
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Bexorad uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your shopping cart
  • Save account preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Display relevant product recommendations
  • Measure marketing campaign performance

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some website features may not function properly.

4. Marketing Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we may send emails regarding:

  • New product launches
  • Exclusive promotions

  • Special discounts
  • Company updates
  • Sports apparel trends

You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the “Unsubscribe” link included in our emails.

We do not store your complete payment card information on our servers. Payments are securely processed through trusted third-party payment providers using encrypted technology that complies with industry security standards.

Accepted payment methods may include:

  • Mastercard

  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Other secure payment providers

To complete your purchase, we collect information necessary for:

  • Returns and refunds

Order information may be retained for accounting, tax, and customer service purposes.

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only with trusted service providers necessary to operate our business, including:

  • Shipping carriers

  • Website hosting providers

  • Customer support platforms

These service providers may only use your information for the services they perform on our behalf.

We may use analytics and advertising tools to better understand website performance and customer behavior. These services may collect information regarding:

  • Website visits
  • Device information

  • Shopping behavior
  • Advertising interactions

This information helps us improve user experience and optimize our marketing efforts.

10. Data Security

Protecting your information is a priority. We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

These measures include:

  • SSL Encryption
  • Secure Payment Processing
  • Restricted Access Controls
  • Firewall Protection
  • Regular Security Monitoring

While we strive to protect your information, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable privacy laws, including the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), you may have certain rights regarding your personal information.

GDPR and UK GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information where legally permitted ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to certain processing of your personal information.
  • Request portability of your personal information in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
  • Object to processing carried out for direct marketing purposes.

If you believe your personal information has been processed unlawfully, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority or supervisory authority in your country of residence.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Know what categories of personal information we collect and how they are used.
  • Request access to the personal information we have collected about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Request deletion of personal information, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Request information regarding the categories of third parties with whom your information has been shared.
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information, where applicable.
  • Not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your privacy rights.

Bexorad does not sell your personal information for monetary compensation. However, certain advertising or analytics technologies may be considered "sharing" under applicable California law. Where required by law, we will provide mechanisms to exercise applicable opt-out rights.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of your applicable privacy rights, please contact us using the contact information provided in this Privacy Policy. We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing your request.

12. Children's Privacy

Bexorad does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that personal information has been submitted by a child without appropriate parental consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information.

13. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Complete transactions
  • Provide customer support
  • Meet legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements
  • Improve our services

When information is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it where appropriate.

14. International Customers

Bexorad serves customers in multiple countries. If you access our website from outside Canada, your personal information may be transferred to, processed, and stored in Canada or other countries where our trusted service providers operate.

Where required by applicable law, we implement appropriate safeguards designed to protect international transfers of personal information, including contractual protections or other legally recognized transfer mechanisms.

Data Controller

For the purposes of applicable privacy laws, including the GDPR where applicable, Bexorad acts as the data controller for the personal information collected through this website.

If you have questions regarding how your personal information is processed or wish to exercise your privacy rights, you may contact us using the details provided in the "Contact Us" section of this Privacy Policy.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom and believe that we have not adequately addressed your privacy concerns, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

If you are a California resident, you may also contact us regarding your rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our business practices, legal requirements, or website functionality. The updated version will always display the latest effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

16. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us.
