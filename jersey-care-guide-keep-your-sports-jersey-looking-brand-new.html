Jersey Care Guide: Keep Your Sports Jersey Looking Brand New

How to Wash and Maintain Sports Jerseys Properly • January 2026 • 6 min read

A premium sports jersey is an investment, and proper care ensures it continues looking great season after season. Whether you wear your jersey every weekend or display it as part of your collection, following the correct maintenance routine will preserve its colors, fabric, and printed details.

Introduction

Taking proper care of your sports jersey helps maintain its appearance, extends its lifespan, and protects printed graphics, logos, and fabric quality. By following a few simple care practices, you can keep your favorite jersey looking as good as new.

Before Washing

  • Always read the care label before cleaning your jersey.
  • Turn the jersey inside out to protect printed graphics.
  • Close all zippers or buttons.

Washing Instructions

  • Wash in cold water.
  • Use gentle detergent.
  • Select a delicate cycle.
  • Wash similar colors together.
  • Avoid fabric softeners when possible.

Drying Tips

  • Air drying is the safest option for maintaining jersey quality.
  • Avoid direct sunlight for long periods.
  • Never use high heat inside a dryer.

Storage Tips

  • Store jerseys on padded hangers or fold them carefully.
  • Keep them away from moisture and excessive heat.
  • Avoid overcrowding your closet.

Removing Wrinkles

  • Use a garment steamer whenever possible.
  • If ironing is necessary, place a cloth between the iron and jersey.
  • Never iron directly over printed numbers or logos.

Common Mistakes

  • Using bleach.
  • Hot water washing.
  • High-temperature drying.
  • Improper storage.
  • Using harsh stain removers.

Long-Term Maintenance

  • Rotate jerseys if you own multiple.
  • Clean stains immediately.
  • Inspect seams regularly.
  • Store collector jerseys separately.

Conclusion

With just a few simple habits, your favorite jersey can stay in excellent condition for years while maintaining its vibrant appearance. Proper washing, drying, and storage will help preserve both the fabric and printed details so you can continue wearing your jersey with pride.
